Research Mentorship Program

A 10-week intensive designed to help high school students think, research, and produce work at a level far beyond their peers.

Program Structure

Week 1 — Foundation

Build your thinking framework. Understand how real research works, choose your direction, and set the base for your project.

Weeks 2–9 — 1:1 Mentorship

Deep work phase. Weekly one-on-one sessions with a mentor to guide your research, refine your thinking, and push your work forward.

Week 10 — Publication & Defense

Finalize your work, prepare it for publication readiness, and defend your research in a structured challenge session.

What You Walk Away With

• A completed research project
• Structured thinking ability
• Mentor feedback at a high level
• Confidence in presenting your work
